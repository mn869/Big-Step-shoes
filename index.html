Our Story

Founded in 2024, Big Step Shoes was born from a simple yet powerful observation: those with larger feet deserved stylish, comfortable footwear options without compromise.

Our founder, Mary Maina, struggled for years to find shoes that combined quality, style, and proper sizing. Tired of settling for whatever happened to be available in his size, he set out to create a brand dedicated to solving this problem.

Today, Big Step Shoes offers an extensive collection of premium footwear specifically designed for those who wear sizes 10-18. We believe that exceptional style shouldn't be limited by size.

Size Guide

Find your perfect fit with our comprehensive sizing information

At Big Step Shoes, we understand the importance of a proper fit. Our shoes are available in sizes 10-18 US, with regular and wide width options.

Use the chart below to find your perfect size. For best results, measure your foot in the evening when feet tend to be at their largest.
